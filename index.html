About Us

We are a start-up specialized in tech education that provide an easy guide for you who want to learn coding online

A11Learn established in 2021, inspired by spider's web that can be found everywhere. We start A11Learn with an aim to help for anyone from any backgrounds who wants to learn coding by creating an easy-to-use online learning platform for coding. Our vision is to give an equal chance for anyone to unleash their skills and potentials especially in this era of digitalization. Our mission is to reach out more people to get to know and learn more about STEM, especially in coding skills that we provide because the web is everywhere!.
